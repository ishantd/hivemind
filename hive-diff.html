Adapting to new age investors and rediscovering growth

Our operations are based on rigorous decision-making frameworks which are driven by the core elements of teamwork, collaboration and communication.

What we do is repeatable and robust but is not dependent on any single process, individual or investment.

This belief extends to every aspect at Hivemind Capital: idea generation to risk management to client servicing. Backed by data, research and a strong commitment towards capital protection, we constantly seek new investment opportunities that will deliver consistent absolute returns for our clients with a margin of safety, following a strong consumer and brand recall.

Where we derive a competitive advantage is through an in-depth and thorough understanding of brands and the Indian market.

That is our road to sustainable and profitable growth by creating long-term value for our investors, our people and our business partners.
